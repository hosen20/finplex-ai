# Human Approval Policy

Finplex AI must not send a customer-facing follow-up automatically. AI may extract invoice data, retrieve evidence, score risk, and draft a suggested message, but a human reviewer must approve, edit, or reject the draft before it is treated as accepted.

Reviewers should confirm that the invoice facts, amount, due date, customer name, and evidence citations match the tenant records. If the evidence is incomplete or there is an open dispute, the reviewer should reject or edit the draft.
