# Regulations And Policy Rules

This folder contains the policy material used by Finplex AI guardrails and RAG evidence. The markdown files are human-readable reviewer guidance. The YAML files in `machine_readable/` are used by automated guardrail checks.

The policies are intentionally local and privacy-safe. They are not legal advice; they document the product rules used to keep AI-generated payment follow-up respectful, evidence-grounded, and human-approved.
