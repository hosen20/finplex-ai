# Regulations And Responsible Follow-Up

Finplex AI supports responsible customer communication for payment follow-up workflows. It does not replace legal review or company policy.

## Product Policy

AI-generated follow-up drafts must be:

- respectful
- evidence-based
- accurate
- non-harassing
- free of unsupported threats
- approved by a human before use

## Guardrail Themes

The guardrails service should check for:

- misleading claims
- aggressive or threatening tone
- unsupported legal statements
- unsupported payment demands
- privacy issues
- missing evidence
- attempts to bypass human review

## Human Review

Every customer-facing draft must be reviewed by a human. The reviewer can approve, edit, reject, or request reprocessing.

## Auditability

Each review decision should record:

- reviewer
- timestamp
- invoice
- customer
- evidence ids
- guardrail result
- final decision
- decision notes

## Disclaimer

This project provides responsible AI workflow support. It is not legal advice and should be adapted to the organization's applicable rules, policies, and jurisdictions before production use.
